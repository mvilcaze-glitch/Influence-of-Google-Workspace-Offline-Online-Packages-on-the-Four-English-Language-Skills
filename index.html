Expected Outcomes

  • 01

    Measurable improvement in listening and reading scores due to offline access and data persistence.

  • 02

    Higher quality in speaking and writing due to asynchronous feedback cycles and revision.

    Student portfolio in Google Drive showing progression from C to AD level across 64 lessons.

  • 04

    Replicable model for other institutions in Arequipa with similar connectivity issues.

Glossary

Key Terms

Essential concepts used throughout the thesis and research program.

O

Offline Mode

Google Workspace feature allowing students to access, edit, and save files without an active internet connection. Data syncs when connection is restored.

A

Asynchronous Sync

The process by which student work created offline (documents, forms, audio) is uploaded to Google Drive at a later time when connectivity is available.

P

Data Persistence

The ability of Google Workspace to retain student responses, drafts, and quiz answers locally until synchronization with the cloud occurs.

R

Receptive Skills

Language skills involving input processing: Listening and Reading. Targeted in HE1 through offline quiz scores and comprehension completion rates.

P

Productive Skills

Language skills involving output generation: Speaking and Writing. Targeted in HE2 through Drive-recorded audio and Google Docs drafts with version history.

C

Cycle VI / VII

Secondary education cycles in the Peruvian curriculum. Cycle VI covers Grades 1-3 (A2 level), and Cycle VII covers Grades 4-5 (A2-B1 level).
